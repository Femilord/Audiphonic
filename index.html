Soundproofing

Noice Isolation and/or Insulation of your space.

Soundproofing is the process of blocking or minimizing the transmission of sound from one space to another. It involves using materials or construction techniques to isolate a space from external noise or prevent internal noise from escaping.

Acoustics

Control Sound reflection(echo) within your space.

Acoustics refers to the science of sound behavior within a space - how it travels, reflects, absorbs, and interacts with surfaces. In Acoustic treatment we focuse on controlling reverberation, echo, and conseqquently - clarity of sound.
