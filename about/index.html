About Us

Audiphonic provides integrated Noise Control and Audio-Visual Systems solutions, shaping quiet, controlled, and high-performance environments through premium soundproofing, acoustics, and AV integration.

With over a decade of industry experience, we support clients who require effective noise control and reliable audio-visual systems. Our work is consistently guided by fitness for purpose, technical excellence, and refined execution.

Our team comprises skilled professionals across every stage of our projects. From administration to technical operations, every member is dedicated to delivering the best client experience.

Our solutions cover all types of environments—from boardrooms and worship auditoriums to recording and broadcast studios. Whatever your space requires, we have the experience and technical depth to provide the best solutions possible.

Driven by a passion for technology, creativity, and design, we deliver exceptional noise-control solutions and integrate high-performance audio-visual systems for flawless experiences in auditoriums, event venues, and workspaces.
