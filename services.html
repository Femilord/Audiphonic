Call Us

When you call us we ebmbark on an assessment of your space

To ensure you receive the best possible outcome, our process begins with understanding your space thoroughly. We achieve this through an on-site assessment and, where access is limited, by reviewing your architectural drawings. In some cases, both may be required to guarantee full accuracy and a perfectly tailored solution.

Site Assessment

Our process begins with a comprehensive site assessment to understand the unique characteristics of your space. This includes observing room layout, structural features, and environmental conditions that may influence performance. By gathering accurate baseline information, we ensure that every noise-control and AV-system recommendation is tailored to your specific needs.

Acoustic and Spatial Analysis Survey

This stage focuses on evaluating the acoustic behaviour and spatial dynamics of your environment. We measure or observe factors such as reverberation, noise sources, material finishes, room shape, and potential reflective surfaces. These insights allow us to determine the most effective soundproofing and acoustic treatment solutions, ensuring that your space delivers clarity, comfort, and control.

AV Infrastructure Evaluation

Our AV infrastructure evaluation examines the technical backbone of your space to determine what is required for seamless system integration. This involves reviewing existing cabling, power supply, equipment placement options, control paths, and structural limitations. With this information, we design an AV system that is reliable, intuitive, and optimized for both current and future use.

Noise Control and Environmental Diagnostic Check

This diagnostic check identifies the internal and external elements that may impact sound quality or contribute to unwanted noise. We assess mechanical systems, adjacent spaces, environmental conditions, and any structural vulnerabilities. The findings enable us to create robust noise-control strategies that enhance performance and prevent disturbances across a wide range of applications.
