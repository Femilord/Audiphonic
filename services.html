Soundproofing

Noice Isolation and/or Insulation of your space

Soundproofing involves integrating a number of noise control solutions for the purpose of reducing or totally removing unwanted noise influence. The two most important modes of tackling noise intrusion is sound isolation and insulation. Isolation involves containing sound generated within your space to your space so as not to disturb the neighbour, while sound insulation has to do with protecting your space from the noise generated from outside of your space. Spaces that generate noise such as Studios, Worship Auditoriums, Concert Halls and Event Centers are always in the need for sound Isolation. The live music from these spaces can constitute a major disturbance for the environment around them. Corporate environments such as Board Rooms, Meeting Rooms, Conference rooms, Offices, Call centers, Recording and Broadcast Studios mostly require more quiet for business operations and therefore need to soundproof their space so as to cut off environmental noise coming from their surroundings. Do contact us for topnotch soundproof solutions.

Acosutics

Acoustics Contain sound reflection(echo) within your space

The acoustics of a space refer to how audio interacts with the properties of the space. Bad spacial acoustics can negatively colour the sound and eventually impact on the message expected to be heard. For environments where meetings are commonly held, speech intelligibility is an important factor which bad acoustics could influence. Such resonant influences as what is commonly referred to as echo create unpleasant feedback on the content of the message being heard. Other environments that require this treatment as an imperative are recording and broadcast studios, Event centers, worship auditoriums, conference halls, concert halls and call centers. Recording studios for example need this to ensure that the audio being captured are devoid of influences from room resonance and frequencies. Facilities such as Worship Auditoriums, Concert Halls, Event centers need acoustics to make the sound as clean and clear as possible for the audience to enjoy. Do contact us for the best solutions for your acoustic challenges
